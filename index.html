Our GLP-1 injection program starts at just $179/month with no hidden fees. This includes your medication, provider consultation, shipping, and 24/7 care support. GLP-1 medications have been clinically proven to help patients lose 15-20% of their body weight. They work by mimicking a natural hormone that regulates appetite and blood sugar. 9 out of 10 patients report it's the most effective treatment they've tried. Our physicians will evaluate your health profile to determine if you're a good candidate.

No insurance is required for our compounded GLP-1 programs ($179/mo injections or $249/mo tablets). These are all-inclusive prices. For brand-name medications, we can help coordinate with your insurance. We also accept HSA/FSA cards as payment.

The most common side effects are mild and typically subside within the first few weeks. These may include nausea, mild stomach discomfort, or decreased appetite (which is actually part of how the medication works). Our providers start you on a lower dose and gradually increase it to minimize side effects. Our care team is available 24/7 if you have any concerns.

After you complete your online visit, our medical providers will review your answers and determine whether treatment may be right for you. If appropriate, you will receive a personalized prescription within 24 hours. Your medication ships free and typically arrives within 3-5 business days. You'll have ongoing access to your care team for dosing adjustments and support.

Treatment duration varies by patient. Most patients see significant results within the first 3-6 months. Our physicians will work with you to create a long-term plan that may include gradual dose reduction as you approach your goal weight. The key is sustainable results — our program includes lifestyle coaching to help you maintain your progress.

Yes. There are no long-term contracts or commitments. Our monthly plan can be cancelled at any time before your next billing cycle. We believe in earning your trust every month. If you choose a quarterly or biannual plan, please review our refund policy for details on early cancellation.
